Camel:

Interesting facts about Camels:

  • Camels ears are furry.

  • Camels can move easily across the sand because of their feet.

  • When they find water they drink a much as possible and store it in their hump.

Chameleon

Interesting facts about Chameleons:

  • Chameleons are reptiles, part of the iguana suborder.

  • Changing skin color is an important part of communication.

  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches.

Interesting facts about Bears:

  • The largest recorded polar bear weighed 1,002 kilograms.

  • Even though bears are big and heavy, they can run very fast.
